Appointment and Consultation Guidelines

1. Appointment Booking

Members may book via:

 - HMO mobile app
 - Call center
 - Accredited clinic front desk

2. Referral Requirement

 - Specialist consultations require a PCP referral
 - Walk-ins without referral may be denied coverage

3. Validity of Approval

 - Appointment approvals are valid for 7 days
 - Expired approvals require reprocessing

4. No-Show Policy

 - Repeated no-shows may result in temporary booking restrictions

5. Teleconsultation Policy

 - Available for primary care and select specialties
 - Subject to plan benefits
