Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

SOP-001: Member Verification

 - Receive member ID and valid government ID
 - Verify eligibility in HMO system
 - Confirm coverage and plan type

SOP-002: Pre-Authorization Processing

 - Receive request from hospital/clinic
 - Validate diagnosis and procedure
 - Check against policy coverage
 - Approve, deny, or escalate to Medical Director

SOP-003: Claims Processing

 - Receive claim documents
 - Validate completeness
 - Match services with coverage policy
 - Approve payment or issue denial reason

SOP-004: Complaints Handling

 - Log complaint in CRM
 - Acknowledge within 24 hours
 - Resolve within 5 business days
