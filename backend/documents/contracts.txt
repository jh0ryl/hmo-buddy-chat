Provider Service Agreement – General Hospitals

Contract ID: HMO-CON-2024-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Expiration Date: December 31, 2026

1. Parties
- This agreement is entered into between ABC Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) and MetroCare General Hospital, an accredited tertiary hospital licensed by the Department of Health.

2. Scope of Services
The Hospital agrees to provide covered medical services to eligible HMO Members, including:
 - Inpatient hospitalization
 - Emergency care
 - Outpatient diagnostic services
 - Surgical procedures covered under the Member’s plan

3. Covered Services
 - Room and board (ward, semi-private, private based on plan)
 - Professional fees (attending physician, surgeon, anesthesiologist)
 - Laboratory and diagnostic imaging
 - Medications administered during confinement

4. Excluded Services
 - Experimental or investigational treatments
 - Cosmetic procedures
 - Non-medically necessary services
 - Services not pre-authorized (except emergencies)

5. Billing and Claims
 - Hospital shall bill ABC HMO directly
 - Claims must be submitted within 60 days from discharge
 - Late submissions may be denied

6. Pre-Authorization
- All non-emergency admissions and procedures require prior approval through the HMO Utilization Management Unit.

7. Termination
- Either party may terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice.
