Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Do I need pre-authorization for emergency cases?
 - No. Emergency cases are covered even without pre-authorization. Notification within 24 hours is required.

Q2: Can I choose any hospital?
 - You may only use accredited HMO partner hospitals unless it is an emergency.

Q3: Are consultations covered?
 - Yes, provided you have a referral from your Primary Care Physician.

Q4: How long does claims processing take?
 - Claims are processed within 30 business days after complete submission.

Q5: What happens if my coverage is exceeded?
 - Any excess amount must be paid by the member.
