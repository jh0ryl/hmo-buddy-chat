HMO Coverage and Benefits Policy

1. Eligibility Policy

Members are eligible for coverage upon:
 - Active employment status
 - Completed enrollment
 - Payment of premiums by employer or member

2. Inpatient Coverage Policy
 - Coverage is subject to plan limits
 - Pre-authorization is required except for life-threatening emergencies
 - Length of stay must be medically justified

3. Emergency Care Policy
 - Emergency services are covered without prior approval
 - Member must notify HMO within 24 hours of emergency admission

4. Outpatient Benefits Policy
 - Consultations require referral from Primary Care Physician (PCP)
 - Diagnostic tests must be conducted at accredited facilities

5. Maternity Coverage Policy
 - Covered after 10 months continuous membership
 - Includes prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care

6. Policy Conflict Rule
 - In case of conflict:
 - Employer Contract
 - Member Contract
 - HMO Policy
 - SOP
